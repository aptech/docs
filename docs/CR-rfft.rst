
rfft
==============================================

Purpose
----------------

Computes a real 1- or 2-D Fast Fourier transform.

Format
----------------
.. function:: rfft(x)

    :param x: 
    :type x: NxK real matrix

    :returns: y (*LxM matrix*), where L and M are the smallest powers of 2 greater than or equal to N and K, respectively.
