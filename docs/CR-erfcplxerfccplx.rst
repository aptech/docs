
erfcplx,erfccplx
==============================================

Purpose
----------------

Computes the Gaussian error function (erfcplx) and its complement (erfccplx) for complex inputs.

Format
----------------
.. function:: erfcplx(z) 
			  erfccplx(z)

    :param z: 
    :type z: NxK complex matrix;  z must be > 0

    :returns: f (*NxK complex matrix*) .
